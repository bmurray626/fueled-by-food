Lunch Menu

The best part of the day typically happens around 11:00 am till 1:00 pm where you get to put down the pens and pencils and pick up a flavorful lunch! We strongly recommend that you find a safe space because all your coworkers are going to want a bite of what we cooked up for you. Take a look at the most popular meals we have below and if you are interested, please fill the form out below to get in contact with one of our associates so we can develop a meal and payment plan that works with your schedule and wallets.

Honey Glazed Chicken with Garlic Green Beans

$5.00

A fan favorite since our food truck days. We grill chicken thighs and then toss them in our secret honey glaze before one last trip to the grill. Served with garlic green beans and you have a meal that mom would be proud of.

Avocado Tuna Melt

$5.00

Some times we need a "crunchy" lunch. Look no farther than our Avocado Tuna Melt. Each sandwich comes with crispy sourdough bread, 1 whole large HASS avocado (none of that tiny avocado stuff in our kitchen), our spin on Tuna salad, and finished off with some sharp cheddar before meeting our sandwich press. Don't worry... we packed extra napkins for you.

BYOP Bowl

$5.00

BYOP Bowl??? Build Your Own Protein Bowl, of course. Each bowl starts with a serving of white or brown rice followed by your choice of fresh veggies and proteins (shrimp, salmon, tuna, chicken, or steak). Top it off with one of our varitey of low-cal sauces for your perfect lunch.

Honey Walnut Spinach Salad

$5.00

Sometimes we just want a salad for lunch because health... or the awesome dinner you will have later on! No need to worry because we won't judge you at all. We start with a bed of fresh Spinach and add whatever toppings your heart desires. We then top it off with some Honey Glazed Walnuts for that extra crunch. Pairs well with our house made vinagerette or ask for other low cal dressings.
